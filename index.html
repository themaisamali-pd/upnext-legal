Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: May 4, 2026

upnext? ("we", "our", or "us") is a student productivity app that helps you manage your class schedule, tasks, and academic workload. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using upnext?, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username (your @handle)
  • Password (stored in encrypted, hashed form — we cannot read it)
  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • School or institution
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Gender (optional)

Schedule & Academic Data
When you use the app, we store:

  • Class names, subjects, and emojis
  • Class times, days, and locations
  • Teacher names and room numbers (if you enter them)
  • Breaks and schedule preferences

Assignments
For each class, we store assignments you create:

  • Assignment title
  • Due date (optional)
  • Obtained and total marks (optional)
  • Graded or non-graded status
  • Completion status (not started, in progress, done)

Quizzes
For each class, we store quizzes you create:

  • Quiz title
  • Date (optional)
  • Obtained and total marks (optional)
  • Completion status (not started, in progress, done)

Class Notes
For each class, we store notes you write. Notes are free-form text and may contain any content you choose to record. Notes are private to your account and are not shared with other users.

Tasks

  • To-do item titles and descriptions
  • Due dates
  • Tags you create

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device & Usage

  • Push notification token (to deliver notifications to your device)
  • Last active timestamp

Camera & Photos
When you use the AI timetable scanner or upload a profile photo, we temporarily process the image. Timetable images are sent to our server for AI analysis and are not stored after processing.

Contacts
With your permission, we access your device contacts solely to help you find friends who are also on upnext?. Contact data is used in-session for matching and is not stored on our servers.

3. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

Your name, @handle, profile photo, and school are visible to other upnext? users when they search for you or view your profile.

With Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

Supabase (supabase.com)
Our database, authentication, file storage, and backend infrastructure provider. All user data, schedules, assignments, quizzes, notes, and tasks are stored on Supabase servers located in Sydney, Australia. Supabase processes data on our behalf under their Data Processing Agreement.

Google (Gemini AI)
When you use the AI timetable scanner, your timetable image is sent via our secure backend to Google's Gemini API for text extraction. The image is used solely to extract class information and is not retained by Google for training purposes under our API agreement. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in these images.

OneSignal (onesignal.com)
We use OneSignal to deliver push notifications to your device. OneSignal receives your device push token and a unique identifier to route notifications. OneSignal's privacy policy is available at onesignal.com/privacy-policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties except as required by law.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of upnext?, our users, or others.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in Sydney, Australia. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Row Level Security (RLS) is enforced at the database level — users can only access their own data
  • Cached data on your device is stored in encrypted storage protected by iOS Data Protection
  • Push notification tokens are stored securely and used only to deliver your notifications

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction — Update inaccurate or incomplete information via your Profile settings at any time.

Deletion — Delete your account from Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data from our servers within 30 days.

Notification Preferences — Disable push notifications at any time in your device Settings or in the app under Profile → Preferences.

Contacts Permission — You can revoke contacts access at any time in iOS Settings → upnext? → Contacts. This only affects friend discovery; all other features continue to work.

Camera & Photos Permission — You can revoke these at any time in iOS Settings → upnext? → Camera / Photos. This disables the timetable scanner and profile photo upload.

To exercise any data rights or submit a privacy request, contact us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

upnext? is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account and associated data.

Users aged 13–17 may use the app with the awareness of their parent or guardian. We do not use data from any users for advertising profiling.

By using the app, you consent to this transfer.
